ACT Teams Championship

23/03/25


The 2025 ACT Teams Rapid Championship is being held on Sunday, 23rd March 2025, at Campbell High. This event is open to all ACTCA members as well as all members of the ACTJCL. The format of the tournament is teams of 4, playing 7 rounds of 15 minute chess. Last years event attracted 20 teams, and a good time was had by all.
