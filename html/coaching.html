ACTJCL Coaching Programs

At the ACT Junior Chess League, we are committed to fostering a love for chess and helping players of all levels develop their skills. Our coaching programs cater to beginners, intermediate players, and advanced competitors alike. Whether you're just starting out or aiming to sharpen your strategies for tournaments, we have a program for you.


Explore our diverse coaching opportunities and discover how we can help you achieve your chess goals. From holiday boot camps to structured weekly classes and school-based programs, ACTJCL provides a range of options tailored to suit your needs. The ACTJCL also offers a program for people who want to coach chess in ACTJCL programs like the Boot Camp, Northside Chess Club, and Intershcool/Afterschool coaching opptunities.

ACTJCL Holiday Boot Camps

The ACTJCL offers chess boot camps during the normally on the second week of all the school holidays. The boot camp consists of four days with a coaching session and 11 round lightning Tournament on the first day. The second day is an Allegro Tournament with a 15+0 time format with 9 rounds with the Allegro tournament also being Australia Chess Federation Rated. The final two days are an intense two days of Long Time / Standard Tournament with a time control of 60 min + 10 seconds. The Long Time Tournament is also an Australia Chess Federation Rated Tournament.

  • Coaching sessions with expert players.
  • 3 Tournaments with varying time controls.
  • Tournaments that are Australia Chess Federation Rated.
