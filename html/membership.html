Join The ACTJCL Today!

Membership of the ACTJCL also entitles juniors to play in two ACT Chess Association (ACTCA) open events per year without having to join the ACTCA. These events include the ACT Lightning Championship, Doeberl Cup, ACT Championship, ANU Open, Vikings Weekender and the ACT Rapidplay Championship.

Membership Benefits

Exclusive Tournaments

Access to members-only tournaments, simultaneous exhibitions with masters, and invitational events that provide competitive experience in a supportive environment.

Expert Coaching

Learn from experienced coaches through group sessions, specialized workshops, and holiday boot camps designed to elevate your game to the next level.

Community Network

Connect with other chess enthusiasts, share experiences, and build lasting friendships through regular meetups, social events, and online forums.

Skill Development

Participate in regular tournaments, workshops, and training sessions to enhance your skills and knowledge of the game.

Ready to Make Your Move?

Join hundreds of young chess players who are already part of the ACTJCL community. Your chess journey starts here!

About us

The ACT Junior Chess League Inc (ACTJCL) is a non profit organisation aiming to promote chess at all levels for all junior players in the ACT and surrounding areas.
