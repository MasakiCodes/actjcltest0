2025 ACT Primary Schools Tuggeranong Zone

Thank you to everyone who turned out for the 2025 ACT Open Tuggeranong Primary Chess Championship. 113 excited chess players took part in the tournament, which was kindly hosted by Trinity Christian School.
Trinity Christian School finished first on 24/28, with St Anthony's in 2nd on 22.5, and Jerrabomberra Primary in 3rd on 21.
The following teams qualify for the final, which will be held on the 29th August at Trinity Christian School
Trinity Christian School (2 teams), St Anthony's (2 teams), Jerrabomberra Primary (2 teams), Wanniassa Hills, St Thomas Kambah. 
The final is a team of 4 event, where each team is paired against another team. Teams are normally selected based on scores in the zone event (top 4 scores in Team A, next 4 in Team B etc), although you can bring a reserve player for each team, and replacement players can also be selected (in case they were unable to attend the zone) There is no charge for the finals.
I would like to thank Trinity Christian School for being such wonderful hosts on the day.

There was one perfect score on the day - Isaac Eldridge (TCS)
