2025 ACT Primary Girls Schools Final

Congratulations to Canberra Girls Grammar School for winning the 2025 ACT Primary Schools Girls Chess Championship. It was a very close event with CGGS finishing on 21.5/28 to take first place. Tied for 2nd place on 21/28 were Rosary Primary and Brindabella Christian College, with Rosary winning the 2nd place trophy on countback.
It was an enjoyable day of chess, with 97 enthusiastic (and well behaved) chess players taking part. The event was hosted by Rosary Primary School, who did a fantastic job of welcoming so many new chess players to the competition.
