2025 ACT Secondary Girls Schools Final

Congratulations to Canberra Grammar School for winning the 2025 ACT Secondary Schools Girls Chess Championship. They scored an impressive 27/28 to take first place, ahead of Merici College on 18/28, and Gold Creek High on 17/28.
It was a wonderful day of chess, with 53 enthusiastic chess players taking part. A big thanks to Merici College who once again did a fantastic job of hosting the event, and in welcoming so many new chess players.

3 players achieved perfect scores and received Perfect Scores medals
Aneke Singh (CGS), Hannah Ni (CGS), Michaella Mackintosh (CGS)
