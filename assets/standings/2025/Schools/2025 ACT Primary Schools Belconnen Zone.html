2025 ACT Primary Schools Belconnen Zone

Thank you to everyone who took part in the 2025 Belconnen Primary Interschool Chess Championship. 26 players took part in the event, which was kindly hosted by St Monica's Evatt.  
Kaleen Primary A finished first on 26/28, with St Monica's A in 2nd on 16, and St Monica's B on 13.5 edging out Kaleen B (also 13.5) for third place on tie-break.
Kaleen Primary (2 teams) and St Monica's (2 teams) qualify for the finals, which will be held in Term 3.

3 players achieved perfect scores : Drew Hansen, Adithi Bolaram, and Daivik Mehul Bhatt (all Kaleen)
