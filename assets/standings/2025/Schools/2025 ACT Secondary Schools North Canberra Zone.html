2025 ACT Secondary Schools North Canberra Zone

Dear chess teachers, thank you for taking part in the 2025 North Canberra Secondary Open Chess Championship. 101 players took part in the tournament that was kindly hosted by Gungahlin College.
First place was taken by Lyneham High A on 23.5/28, ahead of Radford College A (21.5), with Gungahlin College in 3rd place, scoring 20.5
The following teams qualify for the Final, which will be held on Friday, 1st August at Canberra Grammar School.
Lyneham A, Radford A, Radford B,  Radford C, Gungahlin College A, Gungahlin College B, Amaroo A, Amaroo B, John Paul College A, John Paul College B, Gold Creek A,  Burgmann College A, Melba Copland A, Merici College A, Hawker College A
The final is a team of 4 event, where each team is paired against another team. Teams are normally selected based on scores in the zone event (top 4 scores in Team A, next 4 in Team B etc), although you can bring a reserve player for each team, and replacement players can also be selected (in case they were unable to attend the zone)
