2025 ACT Secondary Schools South Canberra Zone

Thank you to everyone who took part in the 2025 South Canberra Secondary Chess Championships. 64 enthusiastic and talented chess players took part in the event that was kindly hosted by Canberra Grammar School. In a very closely contested event Canberra Grammar A came first with 2.15/28, ahead of Telopea Park High and Marist College who tied on 20.5/28.
The following teams qualify for the final, to be held at Canberra Grammar on Friday  2nd August.
Canberra Grammar A, Canberra Grammer B, Canberra Grammar c, Telopea Park A, Telopa Park, B, Marist A, Marist B, Melrose A, Melrose B.
The final is a team of 4 event, where each team is paired against another team. Teams are normally selected based on scores in the zone event (top 4 scores in Team A, next 4 in Team B etc), although you can bring a reserve player for each team, and replacement players can also be selected (in case they were unable to attend the zone)
I would like to thank Canberra Grammar School for being such great hosts, and to all teachers and parents who helped out on the day.
