2025 ACT Primary Schools Central Zone

Thank you to everyone who took part in the 2025 Central/South Canberra Interschool Primary Championships. 66 players took part in a very enjoyable event that was kindly hosted by Canberra Girls Grammar Junior School.
Ainslie Primary finished first on 25/28, with Garran Primary on 21/28 and Canberra Girls Grammar on 18. The following teams qualify for the finals, which will be held in Term 3: Ainslie Primary (2 teams), Garran Primary (2 teams), Canberra Girls Grammar Junior School, Sacred Heart Pearce
The final is a team of 4 event, where each team is paired against another team. Teams are normally selected based on scores in the zone event (top 4 scores in Team A, next 4 in Team B etc), although you can bring a reserve player for each team, and replacement players can also be selected (in case they were unable to attend the zone)
I would like to thank Canberra Girls Grammar Junior School for being such great hosts on the day.

One perfect score was achieved in the competition: Andy Luyen (Ainslie Primary)
