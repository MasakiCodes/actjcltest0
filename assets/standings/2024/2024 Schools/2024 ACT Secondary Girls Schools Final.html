2024 ACT Secondary Girls Schools Final

Congratulations to Canberra Grammar School for winning the 2024 ACT Girls Secondary Schools Chess Championship. They finished on 25 points from 28 games, ahead of Merici College on 18 with Canberra Girls Grammar School in third place on 17.5.
It was an enjoyable day of chess, with 43 enthusiastic players taking part. The event was hosted by Merici College who provided an excellent venue and wonderful hospitality.
There were 2 perfect scores achieved on the day, by Michaella Macintosh (CGS) and Hannah Ni (CGS).
