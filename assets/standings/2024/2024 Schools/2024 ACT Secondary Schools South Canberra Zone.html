2024 ACT Secondary Schools South Canberra Zone

Congratulations to Canberra Grammar School for winning the 2024 ACT Secondary Schools Chess Championship South Canberra Zone. The CGS 1 team finished on 22.5/28, ahead of Telopea Park 1 on 2.5, with CGS 2 finishing 3rd with 18.5 points.
It was an enjoyable day of hard fought chess with 61 players taking part. The top 3 teams qualify for the final (to be held in Term 3) along with Narrabundah College, Alfred Deakin High, Telopea Park 2, Lake Tuggeranong College, and Canberra Grammar 3.
