2024 ACT Primary Girls Schools Final

Congratulations to Canberra Girls Grammar School for winning the 2024 ACT Girls Primary Schools Chess Championship. In a closely contested tournament they scored 22.5/28 to finish ahead of Rosary on 21. There was a tie for third between Kaleen Primary and St Anthony's, with Kaleen winning the third place trophy on countback.
It was a hectic and enjoyable day of chess with 117 players taking part, a new record entry for this event. The event was hosted by Gold Creek School who did a fantastic job of welcoming so many new chess players.
There was one perfect score achieved on the day, by Sahasra Selva (Garran Primary)
