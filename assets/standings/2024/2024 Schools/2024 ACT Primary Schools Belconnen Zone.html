2024 ACT Primary Schools Belconnen Zone

Congratulations to Brindabella Christian College for winning the Belconnen Zone of the 2024 ACT Interchool Primary Chess Championship. The team scored an impressive 27.5/28 to finish ahead of Kaleen Primary School on 19 and St Monicas on 18.5. Also qualifying for the Finals in Term 3 are Macgregor Primary (16.5) and Kaleen 2 (16).
It was an enjoyable day of serious chess, with 61 players taking part. The event was hosted by Macgregor Primary School, who did a fantastic job of welcoming so many new (and experienced) chess players.
There were 3 perfect scores, achieved by Olamide Fasakin (BCC), Ethan Lee (BCC) and Echo Feng (BCC)
