2024 ACT Secondary Schools North Canberra Zone

Congratulations to Lyneham High School for winning the North Canberra Secondary Zone of the ACT Interschool Chess Championship. They scored 25.5/28 to finish ahead of Gungahlin College 18.5 (2nd on tie break) and Lyneham High 2 18.5 (3rd on tie break).
66 enthusiastic chess players took part in the tournament, which was hosted by Gungahlin College.
Apart from the top 3 teams, the following teams have qualified for the finals, which will be held on Monday 29th July at Canberra Grammar School - Radford College, Marist College, Gold Creek High, Burgmann College, Gungahlin College 2, Merici, St Francis Xavier, Melba Copland.

There was 1 perfect score achieved on the day, by Charles Huang (Lyneham High)
