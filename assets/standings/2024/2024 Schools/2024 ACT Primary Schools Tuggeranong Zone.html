2024 ACT Primary Schools Tuggeranong Zone

Congratulations to St Anthony's Primary for winning the Tuggeranong Zone of the ACT Interschool Chess Championships. They scored 24/28 to finish ahead of Wanniassa Hills Primary on 21 and Trinity Christian School on 20.5.
It was an enjoyable (and busy) day of chess, with 117 players taking part. The event was hosted by Trinity Christian School, who did a great job of welcoming so many enthusiastic young chess players.
Apart from the top 3 teams, the following teams have also qualified for the Primary Schools Final: St Anthony's 2, Jerrabomberra Primary, St Thomas the Apostle, Wanniassa Hills Primary 2
